How It Works

From Systemic Biological Debt to predictive, ROI-centric health intelligence—here’s how Burak Multiomics operates.

Problem & Solution

Problem — Systemic Biological Debt: Healthcare remains largely reactive. Unnoticed metabolic irregularities accumulate until they surface as costly chronic crises. Traditional “snapshot” lab reports don’t capture the progression of disease, contributing to unsustainable spending—especially in regions like the GCC where diabetes and metabolic burden are high.

Solution — A Sovereign Bio-Computational Engine: Burak calculates the Velocity of Failure (ΔV) between gut baselines and blood markers to predict metabolic collapse up to 8 weeks before clinical manifestation. We convert fragmented lab data into “Certified Biological Truth,” enabling a shift from reactive spending to predictive, ROI-centric frameworks.

Who It’s Built For

  • Institutional Payers (insurance providers)
  • Government Health Authorities (e.g. Ministries of Health, DHA, M42)
  • Large-scale Clinical Networks

Decision-makers in regional health and institutional risk management are the primary users. Our focus geography is the Doha–Dubai–Riyadh triangle, with scalability across the GCC.

The Burak Engine & Products

The Burak Engine

An automated assembly line for biological data using our proprietary 8-Layer Sovereign Logic. Data flows through a deterministic “Gate and Chute” cascade: ingestion and sanitization (Gate), then progressive clinical and mathematical distillation (Chute) to produce a Velocity of Biological Failure (ΔV) and actionable cohort stratification.

VNRA — Vendor Neutral Reporting Architecture

A “Smart Ingestion Layer” that normalizes heterogeneous data from any regional lab into a unified truth source. It handles 500+ lab-specific nomenclature synonyms, unit normalization, SHA-256 deduplication, and a Zero-PII protocol so data is permanently de-identified while remaining longitudinally traceable.

Population ROI Dashboard

A strategic command center for payers to visualize cohort risk, calculate “Biological Debt,” and track the financial impact of predictive interventions. Outputs include Population Health Index (PHI), Systemic Debt Value, Velocity Heatmaps, and bi-directional drill-down from macro trends to anonymized individual cascades.

Product & Technology

  • SIL (Smart Ingestion Layer): Automated unit normalization and SHA-256 deduplication; Zero-PII at ingress.
  • The Brain (CF2): 7-Layer Cascade Pattern Analysis and 3-out-of-5 Sentinel Rule for cohort assignment.
  • AMS Module: Triage logic for Viral vs. Bacterial inflammatory flashes (e.g. Antimicrobial Stewardship).

Stack: Google Cloud Platform (GCP)—App Engine, Cloud Functions, BigQuery. HIPAA-compliant logic containers; proprietary velocity math (V_G vs V_B) and linear regression slopes. All “Mother of Logic” runs in sovereign GCC-hosted infrastructure—no external black-box AI/ML APIs.
